# shop_management
Shop Management System
This repository contains a Shop Management System, designed to simplify the management of retail shops. The system provides features for handling inventory, sales, customers, suppliers, and employees, offering an all-in-one solution for small to medium-sized businesses.

Features:
Inventory Management: Track products, manage stock levels, and automate reorder processes.
Sales Management: Record sales transactions, generate invoices, and monitor sales trends.
Customer Management: Maintain customer records, track purchase history, and manage loyalty programs.
Supplier Management: Manage supplier information, track orders, and monitor supply chain performance.
Employee Management: Handle employee records, assign roles, and manage work schedules.
Reports & Analytics: Generate detailed reports on sales, inventory, and overall business performance.
